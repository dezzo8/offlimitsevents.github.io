OFF LIMITS

Step into the surreal.

Off Limits is a creative agency specializing in interactive visual experiences that blur the lines between the senses. Blending projection, sound, and real-time interaction, Off Limits transforms reality into a surreal, ever-changing work of art. If you're looking to bring interactive art, projection mapping, or custom audiovisual environments to your own venue, event, or business, we can design and set up a unique experience tailored to your space.
